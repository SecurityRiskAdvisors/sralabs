SRA Labs

SRA Labs is the dedicated research and innovation arm of Security Risk Advisors. This blog serves to showcase the research, capabilities, and talent of our team: practitioners who come from both sides of the spectrum, from red team operators and malware analysts to detection engineers and threat researchers. Our work spans offensive security, application security, reverse engineering, detection engineering, and adversary tradecraft.

Mission and Philosophy

We exist to advance the security landscape by pushing the bounds of offensive and defensive tooling and research. By publishing tools, techniques, and research openly, we aim to raise the collective bar for both attackers and defenders, as well as the many vendors and security providers across the industry.

We are committed to giving back to the security community. Our open-source projects and research are publicly available on GitHub for anyone to use, build on, and learn from.
